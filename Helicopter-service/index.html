About Helicopter Service

One To Another Place With Vaishno Devi Tourism

Vaishno Devi Tourism offers a reliable and comfortable helicopter service for pilgrims visiting the holy shrine of Mata Vaishno Devi. Our helicopter service takes passengers from one location to another, covering the distance between Katra and Sanjichhat in just 8 minutes. With experienced pilots and well-maintained helicopters, we ensure a safe and enjoyable journey. Our helicopter service is designed to offer a hassle-free travel experience, allowing you to focus on your spiritual journey. Book your ride with us for a hassle-free travel experience.
